What we do ?

A youth lead organisation always active and energies to work on social issues, environment protection, youth development, water conservation and theare .

Water Conservation

YPA has taken proactive measures to address the growing challenges related to water scarcity and sustainable water management.

Environment Protection

Founded on the belief that the youth play a pivotal role in shaping the future, the organization focuses on harnessing the energy, creativity, and passion of young individuals to address pressing environmental challenges.

Health & Wellbeing

Committed to making a positive impact on the lives of the youth, the organization focuses on various aspects of health to empower and uplift communities.

Youth Development

Recognizing the immense potential of the youth, the organization focuses on empowering and equipping them with the necessary skills, knowledge, and opportunities to thrive in various aspects of life.

YPA Theatre

Street theatre, being an impactful and accessible form of communication, allows Youth Power Association to reach diverse audiences in urban and rural settings.

YPA Musical Band

Being YPA a youth lead NGO the musical band in our organisation helps talented personality in providing them a platform.

About YPA'sMission

Support & aim nurturing emphatic young leaders and provide opportunities to youth to advance their skills and sharpen their personality making them more equitable, cooperative and sustainable, not just for themselves but also for the society, nation and at global level. YPA, working primarily in environmental, cancer and Youth development, is leaving no stone unturned to ensure enhancement of the living standards of people globally.

Let us know more About YPA

YPA is an NGO working in the field of youth development and empowerment, environment conservation and sustainable development, and water conservation. Along with it, YPA, in co-operation with common people and district administration also works for rectifying other social issues and making people aware of them through public awareness programs.

About YPA'sVision

YPA strives to transform the society into a platform where the youths are capable to understand and recognize their true potential and their rights to the fullest, their duties towards their environment, society and nation, VPA believes in ancient Indian notion of Vasudhaiva Kutumbam, that is, Whole World is One Family and therefore it aims to bring positive changes in the world throughout.
